      Aim of the game
      Copy the displayed sounds by tapping the pads. 
      A new note on the pad is added to the sequence with each round.
      
      How to activate
      Press the START checkbox to start the game and initiate a sequence.
      Copy the rounds.Successfully complete 5 rounds to win the game and get rewarded with a hip drum tune.
      
      Game modes
      POWER Mode: If you make a mistake, there will be a cackle 
      of laughter and you can have another go. 
      If you make a mistake after that then you have lost.           
      STRICT/TRICKY Mode: If you make a mistake, there will be a "NO!" displayed
      and a cackle of laughter and you lose the game.
